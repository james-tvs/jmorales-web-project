The Foxbody Mustang (1979-1993)

The Foxbody Mustang is widely considered the car that saved the Mustang brand. Built on the versatile Fox platform, it provided a lightweight chassis that was perfect for everything from daily driving to professional drag racing.

The most famous variant is the 5.0L V8, which became a cultural icon in the 1980s and early 90s. Its boxy aesthetic and high performance to cost ratio made it a legend among automotive enthusiasts.

Project Car: BluFox

Why Collectors (and me) Love the Foxbody

  • The 5.0 Motor: Famous for its reliability and thump.
  • Light Weight: The notchback models are incredibly light for a V8 car.
  • Aftermarket Support: You can build an entire car from a catalog.
  • Variety: Available in Hatchback, Notchback, and Convertible.
